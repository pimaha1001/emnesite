Ground rules of a book worm...

Our rules are few, but none the less very important in order to have the best book lovers community in the world.

  1. We do not spoil a book for other bookworms, unless we are in a spoilers aloud / conspiraty forum.
  2. We are nice to each other, we spreed postive vibes, and we welcome all opinions, aslong as they are not hurtful towards others.
  3. We are here to inspire, motivate , ask questions and share or love for books with eachother.
  4. We respect different reading tastes — every genre, author, and reading pace is welcome in our book club.
